How Music Has Impacted Me

Music has always been more than just sound to me—it’s a force that has shaped my life in ways I couldn’t have imagined. It’s therapeutic, a way for me to work through emotions and find clarity when I feel overwhelmed. Picking up a guitar or diving into a playlist isn’t just a pastime; it’s a lifeline. There’s something about the rhythm and melody that quiets the noise in my mind, helping me reset and feel like myself again.

Beyond its personal impact, music has given me a social life that I’m incredibly grateful for. It’s connected me with a community of like-minded people who share the same passion for creativity, self-expression, and the energy that music brings to a room. The local NYC scene has been especially transformative for me. There’s an unmatched vibe in the city’s DIY spaces, where people gather in basements, under bridges, or in tiny venues to celebrate music in its rawest form.
